What is Peggle?

Peggle is a whimsical and addictively fun puzzle game developed by PopCap Games. The player is a newly enrolled student in the Peggle Institute, where they will learn the art of Peggling and put their skills to the test through stunning levels and breathtaking challenges.

You're not alone in this adventure—Headmaster Bjorn and other quirky Peggle Masters will assist you with magical powers that help clear the board and rack up high scores.

Gameplay Mechanics

Each level of Peggle consists of a board filled with about 100 colorful pegs arranged in unique patterns. The goal is to hit and clear all orange pegs using a limited number of balls fired from the top of the screen.

The ball bounces off pegs, walls, and objects, following real physics. Some levels contain obstacles, moving parts, and special bricks that require extra strategy.

Peggle Masters

  • Bjorn the Unicorn: Your guide and the first master you meet. His power is the Super Guide, helping you aim perfectly.
  • Jimmy Lightning: A high-energy gopher whose Multiball power adds extra balls to the board, increasing your chances of clearing pegs.
  • Kat Tut: An ancient Egyptian feline queen whose Pyramid power attaches an ancient pyramid to the ball bucket, providing a safety net for missed shots.
  • Splork: An alien with the Space Blast power, which uses advanced technology to light up all nearby pegs, clearing a significant portion of the board.
  • Claude the Lobster: A DJ crustacean whose Flippers power lets you hit the ball like a pinball, bouncing it off flippers to hit more pegs.
  • Renfield the Pumpkin: A spooky character whose Spooky Ball power causes the ball to reappear at the top of the screen after falling out the bottom, giving you another chance to clear pegs.
  • Tula the Sunflower: A cheerful sunflower whose Flower Power lights up 20% of remaining orange pegs, aiding in clearing the board.
  • Warren Rabbit: A magician rabbit whose Lucky Spin power offers a random benefit, such as a Magic Hat, Triple Score, extra ball, or another magic power, adding an element of chance to the game.
  • Lord Cinderbottom: A fiery character whose Fireball power sends a big ball of fire through all pegs, clearing a path and scoring points.
  • Master Hu: A wise owl whose Zen Ball power optimizes and fires an alternative, often better, shot, useful for difficult moves.

Power-ups & Peg Types

  • Orange Pegs: Your main objective. Clear them all to beat the level.
  • Blue Pegs: Regular pegs that give points.
  • Green Pegs: Activate your master’s special power.
  • Purple Pegs: Bonus points galore! They move each turn.

Game Modes

  • Adventure Mode: Progress through levels and meet all the Peggle Masters.
  • Challenge Mode: More difficult variants with tricky goals.
  • Duel: Compete against friends for high scores.

Trivia

  • Peggle was inspired by the Japanese game "Pachinko."
  • The game uses "Ode to Joy" from Beethoven’s 9th Symphony to celebrate victories.
  • Peggle has been released on PC, mobile, Xbox, PlayStation, and more.
